The human family tree, Mitochondrial Eve to today.

A data visualization work in progress by Forrest Oliphant.

Only 10,000 generations (probably less) separate every human from our most recent common ancestor. Your closest love and the worst terrorist on earth, Rush Limbaugh and Noam Chomsky, the cute baby on the tram and that asshole that puked in your apartment's elevator, Osama bin Ladin and the Dalai Lama,,, all of these people are are YOUR cousins, x times removed. Here I try to visualize our family tree.
